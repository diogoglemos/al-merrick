The Happy Family

HAPPY

The Happy is our ultra-versatile performance shortboard model that works in nearly every condition. A favorite among our team riders for its predictability and ease of use.

TWO HAPPY

Building on the success of the Happy, the Two Happy features refined rails and a modified rocker for enhanced performance in more critical waves.

HAPPY EVERYDAY

The Happy Everyday is designed for surfers looking for a board that performs in daily conditions while maintaining the Happy family's signature responsiveness.
