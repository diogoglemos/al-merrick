company

Passion and innovation

Since 1969, Channel Islands Surfboards has been dedicated to performance and quality through out-of-work innovation, and originality. Over the last 50 years, Channel Islands has grown from a local garage-made operation to a worldwide leader in surfboard manufacturing and design. The company continues to drive innovation in surfing and quality in mind and these guidelines have brought us through four decades of constant change in the surf industry. Channel remains and shape the new millennium with innovative design and quality as our main focus.

Generational handcraft

Channel Islands Surfboards was created by Al and Terry Merrick in 1969. From his birth, Al's spent his days in the factory by his board in Santa Barbara. From building model planes to constructing surf shop displays, he learned from his father how to build things with his hands. Through the years, Al has maintained the family tradition of developing high performance board designs collaborating with the world's best surfers like Tom Curren, to Jake Reynolds. Channel Islands continues to evolve with the highest standard of quality.
